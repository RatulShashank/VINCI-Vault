# Extent and beginning of rebalancing.

Market: BTCUSDT
Time-Frame: 1 Hr

As an OB is the movement in the opposite direction just before the supposed trend, (as it makes a better entry for smart money to buy at discount/sell at premium, by manipulating the market, and market usually returns to that place to mitigate the manipulation. So this is exactly shown in this pic.

The pic is 4H. In both the grey and white box, there are only one consecutive opposite candles each(i.e. green in down move and red in up), and those are the mentioned OB.

So market structure could be explained by this.

When looking for balance, market bounces off the OB, and balances the market by dumping the liquidity in opposite direction.

When in first case, market goes beyond the 3rd candle that forms a swing low as it's structure was like that, forming an area of liquidity for market to dump WITHIN the area of OB, and in the second case, market does not go beyond the high of the 3rd candle forming swing high because that is beyond the area of OB.

Another reason could be given  that as market went below that low, that means market will further go below in the future(and it did, further beyond the low of OB, not beyond the Major swing low).

This theory determines market structure to take entry during rebalancing and extent and beginning of rebalancing.
