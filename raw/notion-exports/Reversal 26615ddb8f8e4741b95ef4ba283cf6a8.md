Market: BTCUSDT
Date on chart: March 12, 2024
Time-Frame: Multi

![16](Liquidity%20grab%20and%20dump/image%2010.png)

Market first goes above the high which was Major swing high for the short term basis, BOS up. AND THEN goes below the major swing low of the long term basis. This goes below the long term swing low, without going above the swing high is the reason for trend change. The reason why 1 is not a sign of bearish strength is because before going up, it went below the low in the consolidation, hence going above was inevitable. But it goes below first, that is sign of bullish strength.

Then after that market goes above the high which BOS below, that is 3. And then in right, it goes just below the major swing low of the move that BOS above the move, that is 3, and balances that, then continues up.

Structure similar to the 22 Feb, 2021 one.
