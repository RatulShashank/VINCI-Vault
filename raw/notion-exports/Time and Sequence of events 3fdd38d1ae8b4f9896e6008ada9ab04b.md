# Time and Sequence of events

Market: BTCUSDT
Date on chart: March 12, 2024
Time-Frame: Multi

![](Time%20and%20Sequence%20of%20events/image.png)

This is an important piece of market structure. It emphasizes on the aspect of time's perspective in the market. Take a look at the time when market first BOS up. Market goes up in 2, and then balances the area mentioned in the orange box, below  the low within the ICT displacement range.

But then, after going up beyond 2, it coming back below 3 does not makes sense, unless seen through the lens different perspective. When market went above 2, it BOS up, that means the trend is changed. AND NOW market needs rebalancing the ICT displacement range. Which it does by going below 3, fully balances the orange box, and then finally continues up.

The point is that market should balance within the ICT range before going up, so this is the sequence it did that. As the balance happens AFTER BOS, so that is the premise here.
