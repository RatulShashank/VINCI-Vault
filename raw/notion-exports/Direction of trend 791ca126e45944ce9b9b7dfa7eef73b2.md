# Direction of trend

Market: BTCUSDT
Date on chart: January 19, 2021 12:00 AM (GMT+5:30)
Time-Frame: 1 Day

When a new trend is about to start, market will always fully balance the previous trend completely, i.e. going beyond the last swing point( and bouncing off an area of importance).

There is a strange phenomenon in the market during early 2021 in daily chart. When in a trend, market was always targeting the side in which direction it's going. So, for example, if direction is up, then market will target high of the previous day when in uptrend, and vice versa during down trend.

When in a trend, market continuously forms the candle of the same color. But when it forms that of a different color, then it means two things:-

1) Continuation of the trend better in the direction

2) Trend reversal.

This usually is determined by the direction in which the day of opposite color targets. So for example if the trend is bullish and a red candle forms that targets the high, but not the low, that's telling it's about to be bearish.

But if market targets the low of previous day/days and NOT the high, that means market is going to accelerate in upward direction. That is extremely bullish.
