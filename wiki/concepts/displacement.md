## Definition

**Displacement** is a fast, strong, unidirectional price move that creates FVGs and breaks structure. It is the "delivery mechanism" of the ICT model — the actual move that defines where smart money has entered.

## ICT Displacement Range

The displacement range is the zone measured from the **last major swing level** to the **BOS bar** of the displacement move. Only FVGs and OBs within this range are high-quality rebalancing targets.

## Role in structure

1. Displacement creates a BOS
2. Market enters an OB or FVG **within** the displacement range on the pullback
3. The pullback respects the range — it does not extend beyond it if the structural premise is correct

## Identification

- Strong unidirectional candles with minimal wicks (low wick-to-body ratio)
- Range expansion: candle range significantly above ATR
- Typically accompanied by above-average volume

## Time and sequence

Balancing happens in a specific sequence:
1. BOS occurs (via displacement)
2. Market returns to balance the ICT displacement range (FVG within the range)
3. Only after balance does the move continue

The key insight: the pullback **after** BOS is not a reversal — it is the market completing its obligation to balance the range before the next leg.

## My Take

Displacement range as a filter is extremely useful for reducing noise. "Only FVGs within the range matter" eliminates most false setups. The sequencing concept (BOS → balance → continue) gives a predictable flow that can be coded as a state machine.
